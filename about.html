David Hadden has many business accomplishments, most of which have been related to his work as an automobile mechanic. He has been awarded awards on the local and county level numerous times due to the sincere work he does and the outstanding customer service he continues to provide. David treats every man, woman, and child just as one should: with dignity and respect.

David Hadden is actually a David Hadden Jr. David Roy Hadden jr, to be exact! Dave and his father David are two of a kind: both lifelong mechanics with a passion for doing good, both for family and the community. Some even say Dave is a spitting image of his father David!

David has been with the love of his life Lisa for the better part of two decades. Life has interesting ways of letting people discover love, and David and Lisa's story is no exception. They now reside on their beautiful property in upstate New York with way too many animals, and just enough land for David's liking!
